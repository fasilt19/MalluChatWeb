# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 5, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **MalluChat** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the **MalluChat** mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information:** email address and password when you register, or your Google account information if you sign in with Google.
- **Profile information:** display name, username, profile photo, bio, and other details you add during profile setup.
- **User content:** posts, comments, photos, videos, and messages you create or share in the App.
- **Communications:** messages you exchange with other users through the chat and matching features.
- **Reports:** information you submit when reporting content or users.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data:** features you use, content you interact with, posts you save, and users you match or chat with.
- **Device information:** device model, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Network information:** IP address and connection status (used to maintain real-time chat connections).
- **Push notification token:** a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver notifications to your device.

### 1.3 Permissions We Request

- **Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS):** to send you push notifications about messages, matches, and activity.
- **Photos and videos (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO):** to let you select media from your device to share in posts and chats. We only access the media you choose.
- **Internet and network state:** required for the App to function.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings, though some features may stop working.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account, including email verification and password reset
- Provide core features: chat, matching, posts, explore, comments, and saved posts
- Deliver push notifications about messages and activity (you can disable these in settings)
- Personalize your experience and show you relevant content
- Moderate content, act on reports, and enforce blocking between users
- Maintain the safety and security of the App and its users
- Diagnose problems, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve the App
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

- **With other users:** your profile information, posts, and comments are visible to other users of the App. Messages are visible to the users you chat with.
- **Service providers:** we use trusted third parties to operate the App, including:
  - **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Cloud Messaging) — see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - **Google Sign-In** for optional account login
- **Legal requirements:** we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
- **Business transfers:** if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
- Some data (such as saved posts and cached content) is stored locally on your device to improve performance.
- We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

- We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App.
- When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate purposes such as fraud prevention and dispute resolution.
- Locally cached data can be removed by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App.

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information (you can edit your profile in the App)
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Withdraw consent** for optional permissions (notifications, media access) via device settings
- **Object to or restrict** certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at the email below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

## 7. Blocking & Reporting

The App lets you block other users and report content or behavior that violates our Terms. When you block a user, their content is hidden from you. Reports you submit are reviewed by us and stored to enforce our policies.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. We take steps to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection wherever it is processed.

## 10. Third-Party Links & Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or rely on third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify you within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
