
Create a modern, responsive landing page for **MalluChat** — a social networking and real-time chat app for Malayalis to connect, match, and share moments. The app is built for the Malayalam-speaking community (Kerala and the Malayali diaspora worldwide).

## Brand & Tone

- Name: **MalluChat**
- Personality: warm, friendly, youthful, community-first — with a subtle Kerala flavor (think coconut green, backwater teal, sunset gold accents; do NOT use clichéd stock imagery)
- Tone of copy: casual and inviting, English with an occasional light Malayalam touch (e.g., a headline like "Namaskaram! Your people are here.")
- Style: clean, mobile-first, rounded corners, soft shadows, smooth scroll animations; light theme with dark mode support

## Page Structure

1. **Hero section**
  - Headline: connect with Malayalis around the world
  - Subheadline: chat in real time, match with new friends, and share your moments
  - Primary CTA: "Get it on Google Play" badge (placeholder link)
  - Angled phone mockup showing a chat screen (placeholder image)

2. **Features section** (icon + title + short description, grid of 6):
  - **Real-time chat** — instant one-on-one messaging that feels alive
  - **Match & connect** — discover and match with new people who vibe with you
  - **Share posts** — post photos and videos, and explore what the community is sharing
  - **Save what you love** — bookmark posts to revisit anytime
  - **Stay in the loop** — smart notifications for messages, matches, and activity
  - **Safe community** — report, block, and moderation tools keep the space respectful

3. **How it works** (3 simple steps): Sign up with email or Google → Set up your profile → Start matching, chatting, and sharing

4. **App screenshots carousel** — horizontally scrollable phone frames (placeholder images)

5. **Safety & trust strip** — short section highlighting email verification, blocking/reporting tools, and privacy-first design

6. **Download CTA section** — bold banner repeating the Google Play badge

7. **Footer** — links to Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions pages, contact email (fasilt19@gmail.com), copyright © 2026 MalluChat

## Technical Requirements

- Single-page, fully responsive (mobile-first), semantic HTML
- Fast: no heavy frameworks needed; vanilla HTML/CSS/JS or a lightweight stack is fine
- Accessible: proper contrast, alt text, keyboard navigable
- SEO: meta title ("MalluChat — Chat, Match & Share with Malayalis Worldwide"), meta description, Open Graph tags
- Include two additional simple pages (or routes) rendering the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions from provided markdown content, linked from the footer
- Use placeholder images/mockups that are easy to swap later
